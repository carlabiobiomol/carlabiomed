## 🔬 About me

I'm a final-year Biomedicine student and first-year 
Data Science student, working at the intersection of 
**clinical genomics, bioinformatics and artificial intelligence**.

My research focus is the **Mismatch Repair (MMR) system** 
and its role in **Lynch Syndrome** — exploring how genomic 
data and AI models can support clinical decision-making 
in hereditary cancer.

I also create educational content at 
[@bioinfo.ia](https://instagram.com/bioinfo.ia), making 
bioinformatics accessible to health science students.
